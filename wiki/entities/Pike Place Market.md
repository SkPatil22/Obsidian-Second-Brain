# Pike Place Market

Seattle's landmark public market downtown — 75+ food stalls, fishmongers, flowers, produce, buskers, and water + Olympic Mountain views. The default anchor for a day in downtown Seattle.

## Where to eat
- **Pike Place Chowder** — award-winning, expect a line.
- **Sushi Kashiba** — top-tier sushi; reserve.
- **The Pink Door** — Italian + live music/burlesque; reserve.
- **Beecher's Handmade Cheese** — the mac & cheese.
- **Le Panier** — French pastries (almond croissant).
- **Oriental Mart** (Filipino), **El Borracho** (tacos, veg-friendly), **Market Grill** (salmon sandwich).
