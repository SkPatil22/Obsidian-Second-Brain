# Olympic National Park

Three ecosystems in one park on the Olympic Peninsula NW of Seattle: **alpine mountains, temperate rainforest, and wild Pacific coast**. Best July–Sept (roads open, driest).

## Key areas
- **Hurricane Ridge** — alpine meadows + views, ~17 mi above Port Angeles.
- **Lake Crescent** — Marymere Falls (easy), Mount Storm King (steep). Sunsets.
- **Sol Duc Falls** — short forest hike to a classic waterfall.
- **Hoh Rainforest** — Hall of Mosses; ~4 hr from Seattle (book/arrive early).
- **Rialto / Ruby Beach** — sea stacks, driftwood, tidepools.

## Bases
- **Port Angeles** — best all-rounder (Hurricane Ridge, Lake Crescent, food/lodging).
- **Forks** — closer to the coast + Hoh.

## Planning intel
- 2 days is the sweet spot. One **America the Beautiful** pass covers entry. Cell service poor — offline maps.
