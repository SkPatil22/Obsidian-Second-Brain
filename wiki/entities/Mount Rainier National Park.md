# Mount Rainier National Park

Glaciated 14,411 ft stratovolcano ~2–2.5 hr SE of Seattle. Famous for subalpine **wildflower meadows** and big-mountain hikes.

## Key areas
- **Paradise** (SW) — the headliner. Skyline Trail loop, visitor center, wildflowers. ~2.5h from Seattle via the Nisqually entrance.
- **Sunrise** (NE) — highest road in the park; opens late June/early July with snowmelt. Big Rainier views, fewer crowds.

## Planning intel (2026)
- **No timed-entry reservation required in 2026** — the pilot was cancelled. Just enter.
- **Parking is the real limit:** Paradise fills ~8–9am summer weekends; Sunrise ~10am. **Tue–Thu mornings** best; lots free up ~3–5pm.
- **Wildflower peak: last week of July → first week of August** at Paradise.
- One **America the Beautiful** pass or ~$30/vehicle (7-day).
