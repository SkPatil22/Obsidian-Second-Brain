# Raspberry Chocolate Cake

A deep, moist from-scratch chocolate layer cake with a fresh raspberry filling and a chocolate ganache drip. Built on the canonical "add-boiling-water" chocolate sponge (oil + hot coffee = reliably moist) and the techniques in [[Baking - Berries and Moisture]].

> [!tip] Read first
> The two things that make or break this cake — keeping the crumb moist and keeping raspberries from sinking/bleeding — are covered in [[Baking - Berries and Moisture]]. The short version is baked into the steps below.

## Ingredients

**Chocolate sponge** (three 8" layers)
- 2 cups (400 g) granulated sugar
- 1¾ cups (220 g) all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup (65 g) unsweetened cocoa powder (Dutch-process for darker color)
- 2 tsp baking soda · 1 tsp baking powder · 1 tsp salt
- 2 large eggs, room temp
- 1 cup (240 ml) buttermilk, room temp
- ½ cup (120 ml) neutral oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup (240 ml) hot brewed coffee (or boiling water)

**Raspberry filling**
- 12 oz (340 g) raspberries (fresh or frozen) + ¼ cup (50 g) sugar + 1 tbsp lemon juice + 1 tbsp cornstarch (in 1 tbsp water)

**Chocolate buttercream**
- 1½ cups (340 g) unsalted butter, soft · 4 cups (480 g) powdered sugar · ¾ cup (65 g) cocoa · pinch salt · 2–3 tbsp cream · 1 tsp vanilla

**Ganache drip**
- 4 oz (115 g) dark chocolate, chopped · ½ cup (120 ml) heavy cream (high-fat) · fresh raspberries to decorate

## Method

1. **Prep.** Heat oven to 350°F / 175°C. Grease + parchment three 8" pans. Brew the coffee.
2. **Filling.** Simmer raspberries, sugar, lemon juice ~5 min, mash. Stir in cornstarch slurry, cook 1–2 min until thick (coats a spoon). Cool completely — it must be cold and set before assembly.
3. **Batter.** Whisk dry ingredients. Add eggs, buttermilk, oil, vanilla; mix 2 min. Stir in hot coffee last — **batter will be thin, that's correct** (this is what makes it moist).
4. **Bake** 33–37 min until a pick comes out with a few moist crumbs. Cool in pans 10 min, then fully on a rack. **Chill layers** ~30 min before assembling (firm = clean stacking).
5. **Buttercream.** Beat butter 3 min; add sugar+cocoa+salt; add cream/vanilla; whip 3–4 min until fluffy.
6. **Assemble.** Layer → ring of buttercream as a dam → spoon raspberry filling inside the dam → repeat. Crumb-coat, chill 1 hr (see ganache note).
7. **Ganache drip.** Heat cream, pour over chocolate, rest 2 min, stir smooth. **Cool to barely warm.** With the cake cold from the fridge, spoon drips down the chilled edge, fill the top, crown with fresh raspberries.

## Notes / variations
- **Raspberries in the sponge** (not just filling): use **frozen, unthawed**, tossed in 1 tbsp flour, folded in last — see [[Baking - Berries and Moisture]].
- Make-ahead: layers freeze well wrapped; filling keeps 3 days chilled.
