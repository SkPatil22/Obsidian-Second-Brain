# Thin Ribeye Recipes — Healthy & Portable

Thin ribeye (1/4"–1/2" thick) cooks in under 5 minutes total and delivers 40–64g protein per serving. The fat marbling means it doesn't dry out on reheat — making it one of the best cuts for pack-ahead meals. The key for keeping it healthy: skip the butter baste, use a high-smoke-point oil, and lean on acid/herb sauces that don't get soggy in transit.

---

## Cooking the Steak (universal base)

1. Pat completely dry — moisture kills the crust.
2. Season salt + pepper (or marinade; see below).
3. Cast iron or carbon steel, ripping hot. Avocado oil (high smoke point).
4. **2–3 min/side** for medium-rare, **3–4 min/side** for medium. Don't move it.
5. Rest 3–5 min on a board, then **slice against the grain** — thinner slices = more tender.

Nutrition baseline (6 oz ribeye, no sauce): ~400 cal · 42g protein · 24g fat · 0g carb.

## Recipe 1 — Korean Bulgogi-Style Bowl ⭐ Best for Meal Prep

**Pack score:** ★★★★★ — tastes great cold; sauce doesn't separate.

**Marinade** (30 min minimum, overnight is better):
- 3 tbsp soy sauce · 1 tbsp sesame oil · 2 cloves garlic, grated · 1 tsp fresh ginger · 1 tsp brown sugar · 2 tbsp grated asian pear or apple (natural tenderizer)

**Cook:** 2 min/side (marinade has sugar — watch for char).

**Pack with:** steamed white or brown rice, quick-pickled cucumber (rice vinegar + salt, 15 min), sliced scallions, sesame seeds.

**Keeps:** 4 days fridge.

---

## Recipe 2 — Chimichurri Steak + Farro Bowl

**Pack score:** ★★★★☆ — oil-based sauce = no sogginess; farro holds shape.

**Chimichurri** (make ahead, keeps 1 week in the fridge):
- 1 cup packed flat-leaf parsley · 3 cloves garlic · 2 tbsp red wine vinegar · 1/2 tsp chili flakes · 1/2 tsp salt · 1/3 cup olive oil. Pulse, don't blend smooth.

**Cook:** plain salt + pepper steak; chimichurri goes on after slicing.

**Pack with:** farro or brown rice, roasted bell peppers, optional sliced avocado (add day-of).

**Keeps:** 4 days fridge (avocado separate).

---

## Recipe 3 — Thai Steak Salad Bowl

**Pack score:** ★★★☆☆ — bright and fresh; pack sauce separately to preserve crunch.

**Sauce:** 2 tbsp lime juice · 1 tbsp fish sauce · 1 tsp chili flakes · handful cilantro + mint · 1/4 red onion, thin-sliced.

**Cook:** plain or with just salt. Toss sliced steak in sauce immediately off the board.

**Pack with:** shredded cabbage + quinoa base (more stable than leafy greens), cherry tomatoes.

**Keeps:** 3 days fridge (sauce separate).

---

## Recipe 4 — Steak Stir-Fry (Single Skillet, 30 min)

**Pack score:** ★★★★☆ — single pan, everything melds overnight.

**Sauce:** 3 tbsp soy sauce · 1 tbsp oyster sauce · 1 tsp ginger paste · 1 tsp garlic paste · 1 tsp honey · 1 tsp cornstarch (thickens).

**Veg:** broccoli florets, snap peas, bell pepper, mushrooms — high heat, 4–5 min.

**Method:** cook veg first, remove; sear steak slices (thin = 60 sec/side); return veg, add sauce, toss 30 sec.

**Pack with:** brown rice or cauliflower rice.

**Keeps:** 4 days fridge.

---

## What Not to Pack

- **Creamy sauces** (béarnaise, cream-based) — break and get slimy.
- **Leafy greens as the base** — wilt by day 2. Use cabbage, quinoa, or rice instead.
- **Un-rested steak** — cutting too early leaks juice and makes everything soggy.
